Testimonials form some of our past residents

A great place to stay

"My wife and I stayed two nights here and had the ensuite room on the top floor with use of a lounge and kitchen but as we were the only guests we had the whole apartment to ourselves. The location is ideal right in the middle of town with a good view of the local streets."

A home away from home

we spent 5 nights at Gannon's during the Arts festival and simply couldn't imagine a better arrangement for us. We shared the upper quarters (two guest rooms and shared kitchen/living areas) with friends of ours so it felt like a private apartment. We had everything we needed provided--coffee, tea, snacks, breakfast fixings--and if we wanted to stock up on anything else, the grocery store was directly across the street. We didnt have daily maid service, but we werent expecting it, so that's just an FYI for others. Two flights of stairs to get to our unit meant plenty of privacy. Gerry was super friendly and accomodating, but not in your face. Stayed September 2014, travelled with friends

Really lovely B and B and super host

We booked for two nights and stayed three. Great location in Cliften and Gerry runs a very good B&B. The rooms are beautifully furnished and the common areas very comfortable with big soft sofas. The breakfast is continental and a nice change from the Irish cooked breakfast, which we also enjoy. We can recommend the scones too - very tasty and served hot from the oven. No complaints and would return again. Stayed September 2014, travelled as a couple
