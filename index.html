Who we are

Welcome to Galway Bay B and B. You are in for a wonderous stay when you choose to stay with us. We are located on the west coast of Ireland in Galway City. We offer spectacular views over looking the Beautiful Galway Bay and we are right beside the hustle and bustle of the lively main streets of Galway.

Myself and my husband have been in operation for the last 20 years and have provided our customers with the best say possible each and every time.
